CYBER SECURITY

Cybersecurity involves protecting computers, networks, and digital systems from unauthorized access, damage, or theft of data. It encompasses various practices and technologies that aim to safeguard information and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of digital resources.

Key Concepts in Cybersecurity

  1. Authentication

    Authentication is the process of verifying the identity of a user, device, or system. It ensures that only authorized individuals or entities can access sensitive information or perform specific actions.

  2. Firewalls

    Firewalls are network security devices that monitor and control incoming and outgoing network traffic. They act as a barrier between trusted internal networks and external networks, preventing unauthorized access and potential attacks.

  3. Encryption

    Encryption is the process of encoding data in a way that can only be accessed or decoded by authorized individuals or systems. It helps protect sensitive information from unauthorized disclosure or modification.

  4. Malware Protection

    Malware refers to malicious software that can harm or compromise computer systems. Malware protection involves using antivirus software, regularly updating software and operating systems, and being cautious of suspicious email attachments or downloads.
